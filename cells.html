Why do cells split?

Context

We were asked to take redesign visuals and present a way of teaching Surface Area and Volume ratio to kids and use a real-life example to strengthen the child's learning. The target age group was for kids in middle school.

Making

With this project came a big realization, illustrations used in textbooks haven't changed in years and they all are similar. The academic structure has undergone varies changes, this means the design of textbooks and illustrations should as well. This project was fun and I wanted the design to project that. I first worked on visuals like illustrations and color. I felt the best way to maintain a child's interest would be through an interactive textbook and by posing questions, the child would be forced to think. I wanted to retain particular features of an ordinary textbook as well. I added a highlighter feature to the design so that the child can create notes as per their liking. Notes I believe are unique to each individual. This project was stimulating and fun.
