Tikisha

Context

The Creative Coding Club at NC State was having an audio-visual performance at Gregg Museum at the begining of December. Using processing we were to create visuals which responded to music (sound).

Making

I wanted to create something visually colorful and the idea of a face talking to the audience as the music played, was intriguing to me. After a couple of iterations, I finalized on the visual. Since I don't really know about creating music, David Storelli from the Creative Coding Club created this amazing music score for the visual.
