Wild

Context

We were told to explore a section of the NC State library and find a book whose design attracted us. After selecting the book, we were asked to design and make a book based on the selected book.

Making

The section of the library I was exploring had old and oversized books which were fascinating to say the least. I narrowed down on a wildlife photographer's portfolio which was nothing short of genius. The way it blended art, simplicity, and animals was beautiful. Keeping that in mind, I made a book to depict how animals have been influencing art forms. I covered wildlife photography, literature, and animation. The content of the book was curated from material I gathered from other books by Disney's animators, authors such as Rudyard Kipling, and other wildlife photographers such as James Balog. Though the most intimidating portion of it all was the printing and binding process, I needed to be as careful as possible. Nonetheless seeing the pages form into a book was mindblowing.
